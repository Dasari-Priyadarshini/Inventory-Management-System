About

The Inventory Management System is a web-based software application developed to help businesses manage their products and stock easily and accurately. Many shops and warehouses struggle with handwritten records, missing data, and manual calculations. This system solves those problems by storing and updating product information digitally.

Using this system, users can add new products, update stock quantity, record sales, and check availability at any time. Whenever a sale is made, the stock is automatically reduced, and low-stock alerts help business owners refill items on time. The system is built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and MySQL to ensure a smooth, fast, and user-friendly experience.

In simple words, the Inventory Management System acts as a smart digital assistant that keeps products organized, saves time, reduces errors, and makes business management easier.

Technology

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js (Express), MySQL
